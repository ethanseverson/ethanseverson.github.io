How to use Ethan's OneDrive Video Tool

 

 

Step 1: Upload Video to OneDrive

 

 

Visit https://onedrive.live.com and sign in.

 

Create a new folder called Game Clips.

 

Once in the new folder, click upload, then upload files.

 

 

Step 2: Create a sharable embeded link

Right click the video you just uploaded and click “Embed”

 

Then click generate

 

Copy the text to your clipboard

 

 

Step 3: Open my tool by double clicking the .jar

 

Step 4: Use the tool

The tool will automatically paste the embedded link you copied into the input box.

Click Generate Direct Download Link to get a direct URL to the video uploaded.

 

Step 5: Copy/Paste to Discord

 

Simple as that.
